<section class="home-hero">
  <p class="home-kicker">Postdoctoral Researcher in Computer Science</p>
  <h1>Junjie Wu</h1>
  <p>
    I am a postdoctoral researcher at Southwest Jiaotong University. My research
    focuses on AI-empowered next-generation wireless networks, with emphasis on
    wireless resource management, intelligent optimization, and generative AI for
    networking.
  </p>
  <p>
    My work sits at the intersection of deep learning, wireless communications,
    and large language models, aiming to make future wireless systems more
    efficient, adaptive, and intent-aware.
  </p>
</section>
