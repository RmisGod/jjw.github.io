I am currently a **Postdoctoral Researcher** at **Southwest Jiaotong University**, having recently completed my Ph.D. in **Information and Communication Engineering**. My research focuses on **AI-empowered next‑generation wireless networks**, with particular emphasis on **wireless resource management, intelligent optimization, and generative AI for networking**.

My work sits at the intersection of **deep learning, wireless communications, and large language models (LLMs)**, aiming to make future wireless systems more efficient, adaptive, and intent‑aware.

---

## Research Interests
- AI for Next‑Generation Wi‑Fi / Wireless Networks  
- Generative AI (Diffusion Models, LLMs) for Resource Allocation  
- Multi‑Agent Deep Reinforcement Learning (MADRL)  
- Federated Learning for Distributed Wireless Systems  
- UAV‑Assisted and Cooperative Wireless Networks
